War Thunder offers a vast selection of naval vessels from 10 nations, including the USA, Germany, Soviet Union, Britain, Japan, and more. Players can choose from a wide variety of ships, ranging from early World War II destroyers to modern warships.

Most Popular Nations The most popular naval nations are USA and Japan. The U.S. features iconic ships like the USS Fletcher, known for its durability and versatility, while Japan offers renowned vessels such as the IJN Yamato, famous for its formidable firepower and resilience.

Naval Ships As players progress, they can unlock modern destroyers like the USS Sumner from the USA and swift torpedo boats like the IJN Fubuki from Japan. These vessels offer fast-paced naval combat with powerful armaments and require advanced tactics to dominate the seas. With so many nations and ships to choose from, War Thunder has a vessel for every type of captain, whether you enjoy large-scale battleships or swift torpedo warfare!
