War Thunder Tank Overview

War Thunder offers a vast selection of tanks from 10 nations, including the USA, Germany, Soviet Union, Britain, Japan, and more. Players can choose from a wide variety of vehicles, ranging from early World War II tanks to modern armored vehicles.

Most Popular Nations The most popular tank nations are USA and Germany. The U.S. features iconic tanks like the M4 Sherman, known for its versatility and effectiveness in various combat situations, while Germany offers renowned vehicles such as the Tiger I, famous for its formidable firepower and armor.

Tank Combat As players progress, they can unlock modern tanks like the M1 Abrams from the USA and the Leopard 2 from Germany. These armored vehicles offer intense ground combat with powerful cannons and require advanced tactics to dominate the battlefield. With so many nations and tanks to choose from, War Thunder has a vehicle for every type of commander, whether you enjoy heavy armor assaults or fast, mobile warfare!
